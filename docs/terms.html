2. What the Patent Analyzer Prompt System Is

The Patent Analyzer Prompt System is a free, open-source collection of 6 AI prompts designed to be copy-pasted into AI chatbots (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, etc.) to produce patent feasibility analysis. The prompts guide the AI through technical intake, prior art research, patentability assessment, deep dive analysis, IP strategy, and final report generation. This is a research and educational tool — it is not a law firm, not a patent search service, and not a substitute for professional legal counsel.
